Introduction to Design and Analysis of algorithms

Course Introduction

This course forms the backbone of computer science and computational problem-solving. Algorithms are the key to developing efficient and reliable solutions to problems across various domains, from software engineering to data science and artificial intelligence.

We explore a wide range of algorithmic paradigms, such as:

  • Divide and Conquer
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Backtracking
  • Graph Algorithms

This course delves into the principles of algorithm design and analysis while emphasizing practical applications in solving real-world problems. Real-World Applications:

  • Search and Optimization: Algorithms used in search engines, logistics, and supply chain management.
  • Graph Algorithms: Applications in navigation systems like google maps.
  • Dynamic Programming: Solutions for resource allocation, financial modeling, and bioinformatics.
  • Greedy Algorithms: Scheduling problems, route optimization, and streaming services.

Algortihms are everywhere! and thus we use them in our smart city design.
